Welcome to Jazz4Wellness

Mission Statement

Helping people lead healthy lives through live jazz music

Jazz4Wellness is a community service program. It was born from a personal experience of visiting loved ones in hospitals and rehabilitation centers. The idea is that music therapy can promote levels of wellness. There have been several studies where music therapy provided levels of aid to cancer patients and patients with ADD and other types of patients. Music is being used in hospitals to promote relaxation, lower blood pressure, reduce stress and bring on a more positive state of mind. Jazz4Wellness is a MA metro west service where jazz performing artists or jazz bands will come to your facility and play jazz music for your patients and residents.
